### 2. The Portfolio Strategy: 6 AAA Shows

With 6 shows in the library by Year 3, your **vZero Publisher** entity becomes a "Volumetric Netflix."

* **The Rotation:** Flagships run the "New Release" for 6 months (Prime Time). The 5 previous shows move into "Legacy Slots" (mornings or late nights), allowing you to maximize every hour of the venue's operation.
* **The Revenue Hedge:** If Show #4 (a Rock legend) isn't hitting the numbers in Tokyo, you can instantly pivot that venue to Show #2 (a K-Pop star) with zero downtime, as the hardware is identical.
* **Production Cost:** At $10M per show, you have invested **$60M in content** by Year 3. However, since the same 4DGS (Gaussian Splat) files are used for Flagships, Licensed LBEs, and Home Viewing, your **Revenue-per-Asset** is massive.

### 3. Scaling the Publisher: The B2B Network

While you manage 8 flagships, your **vZero Publisher** licenses those 6 shows to **250+ third-party LBE locations** worldwide (Standard VR arcades).

* **Model:** You provide the "vZero Play" software. They provide the Steam Frame headsets.
* **Economics:** $5.00/ticket royalty. If 250 sites average 10,000 visitors a year for your content:
* **Annual Licensing Income:** **$12.5M** of nearly pure profit.
