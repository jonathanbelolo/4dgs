## I. The Capital Strategy: The "Dual-Tranche" Raise

We separate our capital needs into two distinct "buckets" to minimize equity dilution and maximize cash flow efficiency.

### 1. Equity Capital (Series A: $40M)

* **Purpose:** Funding the **vZero Publisher** and **vZero Retail** "Proof of Concepts."
* **Allocation:** Content production ($15M), Flagship 1 & 2 build-outs ($21M), Core R&D ($4M).
* **Target Multiplier:** We seek a 7x–12x ARR multiple based on our proprietary 4DGS library.

### 2. Structured Debt (The SPV Facility: $100M+)

* **Purpose:** Funding the **vZero Home** (Neural Pass) hardware rollout.
* **Mechanism:** We utilize a **Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV)** known as "vZero Finance Co." This entity is legally separate from the main company.
* **The Transaction:** A banking partner (e.g., Goldman Sachs, HSBC) provides a credit line to the SPV. The SPV buys the Steam Frames from Valve and "leases" them to our subscribers.
* **Benefit:** The $100M+ debt stays off vZero’s main balance sheet. The main company remains a "Light" software-and-media entity with high valuation multiples.

## II. Unit Economics: The "Neural Pass" (24-Month Term)

Our subscription model is designed to reach "Cash-Neutrality" in Year 1 and "High-Yield" in Year 2.

| Metric | Amount | Description |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Upfront Hardware COGS** | ($1,100.00) | Steam Frame, Vest, Mods, & Shipping. |
| **Monthly Subscription** | $49.99 | Baseline Neural Pass. |
| **Monthly Avg. Add-on** | $15.00 | Expansion Packs (Sports, Gaming, Wellness). |
| **Total Monthly Revenue** | **$64.99** | **Gross Recurring Revenue per User.** |
| **Direct Operating Cost** | ($8.00) | Cloud Streaming, Licensing, & Support. |
| **SPV Debt Service** | ($22.00) | Interest + Principal repayment to the bank. |
| **Net Monthly Margin** | **$34.99** | **Free Cash Flow back to vZero.** |

**The "Cliff" Effect:** At Month 25, the debt is retired. The $22.00/mo debt service disappears, and the hardware is fully depreciated. **The Net Margin jumps to ~$56.00/mo per user (86% Margin).**

## V. Risk & Sensitivity Analysis

* **Hardware Default:** If a user stops paying, the Steam Frame is remotely bricked. The SPV has a "Right of Repossession."
* **Churn:** We mitigate churn by bundling "The Big 6" residencies. If a user cancels, they lose access to exclusive artist content they cannot get anywhere else.
* **Interest Rate Risk:** Our SPV facility will use fixed-rate hedging to ensure a spike in global rates doesn't crush our subscription margins.
