### 2. The Staggered 2-Room Model (OpEx vs. Capex)

You asked how many rooms find the "sweet spot." **Two rooms is the mathematical pivot point** for efficiency.

#### **The "Staffing Synergy"**

One of the biggest costs in LBE is **human labor**. With one room, your staff is "busy" only 10% of the hour (during onboarding/offboarding). With two rooms, you maximize their time.

* **Room 1:** Starts at **:00**. Staff spends 10 mins onboarding.
* **Room 2:** Starts at **:15**. Same staff moves to Room 2 for a 10-min onboard.
* **Result:** You double your revenue ($200/hr  $400/hr) while only adding **one** extra technician to monitor the server rack.

#### **Capex vs. Scaling**

As we discussed, you don't double the Capex because:

1. **Shared Server Rack:** One massive **Nvidia Rubin/60-series rack** can handle both rooms' compute requirements ($2.8M total vs. 2x $1.8M).
2. **Shared Content:** The **$5M 4D Gaussian Splatting** project is a one-time cost. Whether you have 1 room or 100, the artist only gets paid (and captured) once.
